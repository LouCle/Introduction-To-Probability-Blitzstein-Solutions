4.3.1 problem 38

Let Ij be the indicator random variable for j-th person drawing the slip of paper containing their name.

Let X = ∑ ⁡ j=1nI j be the number of people who draw their name. Then, by linearity of expectation, E(X) = E(∑ ⁡ j=1nI j) = ∑ ⁡ j=1nE(I j) = ∑ ⁡ j=1n1 n = 1.
