3.1.5 problem 5

(a)
p(n) is clearly non-negative. Also,

∑ n=0∞p(n) = 1 2 ∑ n=0∞ 1 2n = 1 2 ∗ 1 1 − 1 2 = 1.

Thus, p(n) is a valid PMF.

(b)
F(x) = ∑ n=0⌊x⌋p(n) = 1 2 ∑ n=0⌊x⌋ 1 2n = 1 2 ∗ 1 − 1 2⌊x⌋+1 1 − 1 2 = 1 − 1 2⌊x⌋+1

for x ≥ 0 and 0 for x < 0.
