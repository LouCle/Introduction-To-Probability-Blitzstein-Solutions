1.5.1 problem 49

Let Ai be the event that i is never rolled for 1 ≤ i ≤ 6. The event of interested then is ⋃ ⁡ i=16A i.

By inclusion-exclusion, P(⋃ ⁡ i=16A i) = ∑ ⁡ i=16P(A i)−∑ ⁡ 1≤i<j≤6P(Ai∩Aj)+∑ ⁡ 1≤i<j<k≤6P(Ai∩Aj∩Ak)−⋯−P(⋂ ⁡ i=16A i).

Now,

P(Ai) = 5n 6n = (5 6)n

P(Ai ∩ Aj) = 4n 6n = (4 6)n

P(Ai ∩ Aj ∩ Ak) = 3n 6n = (3 6)n

P(Ai ∩ Aj ∩ Ak ∩ Aw) = 2n 6n = (2 6)n

P(Ai ∩ Aj ∩ Ak ∩ Aw ∩ Az) = 1n 6n = (1 6)n

P(⋂ ⁡ i=16A i) = 0

Thus, P(⋃ ⁡ i=16A i) = 6(5 6)n −( 6 2)(4 6)n +( 6 3)(3 6)n −( 6 4)(2 6)n +( 6 5)(1 6)n
