(b)
Let Ij be the indicator variable for person j in the sample being a member of party A. Then, X = ∑ ⁡ i=1mI i⇒E(X) = mnA n by symmetry.

Var(X) = E(X2) − (E(X))2 = ∑ ⁡ i=1nA(i2(nA i)( n−nA m−i) ( n m) ) − (mnA n )2
