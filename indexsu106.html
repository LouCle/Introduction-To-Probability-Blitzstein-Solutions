(b)
Let I ⊂ X be the subset of the support where P1(x) < P2(x). Then ∑ x∈XP1(x) = ∑ x∈IP1(x)+∑ x∈X∖IP1(x) < ∑ x∈IP2(x)+∑ x∈X∖IP2(x) = 1.

Thus, having such a property in PMFs is impossible.
