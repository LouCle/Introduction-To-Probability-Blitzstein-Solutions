2.3.3 problem 43

Let Ci be the event that Door i contains the car. Let Di be the event that Monty opens Door i. Let Oi be the event that Door i contains the computer, and let Gi be the event that Door i contains the goat.

(a)
P(C3|D2,G2) = P(C3)P(D2,G2|C3) P(D2,G2) = P(C3)P(D2,G2|C3) P(C3)P(D2,G2|C3) + P(C3c)P(D2,G2|C3c) = 1 3 ∗ 1 2 1 3 ∗ 1 2 + 2 3P(G2|C3c)P(D2|G2,C3c) = 1 3 ∗ 1 2 1 3 ∗ 1 2 + 2 3 ∗ 1 4 = 1 6 1 6 + 1 6 = 1 2.
(b)
P(C3|D2,O2) = P(C3)P(D2,O2|C3) P(C3)P(D2,O2|C3) + P(C3c)P(D2,O2|C3c) = P(C3)P(D2,O2|C3) P(C3)P(D2,O2|C3) + P(C3c)P(D2,O2|C3c) = 1 3P(O2|C3)P(D2|O2,C3) 1 3P(O2|C3)P(D2|O2,C3) + P(C3c)P(D2,O2|C3c) = 1 3 ∗ 1 2 ∗ p 1 3 ∗ 1 2 ∗ p + 2 3 ∗ 1 4 ∗ q = 1 6p 1 6p + 1 6q = 1 2p.
