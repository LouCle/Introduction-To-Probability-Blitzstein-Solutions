4.3.13 problem 53

Let Ij be the indicator variable for tosses j and j+1 landing heads for 1≤j≤3. Then, the expected number of such pairs is E(X)=∑3j=1P(Ij=1)=3p2. Var(X)=E(X2)−9p4.

E(X2)=E((∑3j=1Ij)2)=E((I1+I2)2+2(I1+I2)I3+I23)=E(I21+2I1I2+I22+2I1I3+2I2I3+I23). Note that I2j=Ij. Thus, E(X2)=(p2+2p3+p2+2p3+2p3+p2)=6p3+3p2.

Thus,

Var(X)=6p3+3p2−9p4
