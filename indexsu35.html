1.5.2 problem 52

Let Ai be the event that the i-th student takes the same seat on both days. The desired probability then is 1 − P(⋃ ⁡ i=120A i). By inclusion exclusion principle,

P(⋃ ⁡ i=120A i) = ∑ ⁡ iP(Ai)−∑ ⁡ i<jP(Ai∩Aj)+∑ ⁡ i<j<kP(Ai∩Aj∩Ak)−⋯+(−1)21P(A 1∩⋯∩A20),
where P(Ai) = 19! 20!, P(Ai ∩ Aj) = 18! 20! and so on by naive definition of probability.
