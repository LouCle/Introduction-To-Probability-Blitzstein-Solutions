1.1.12 problem 14

There are 4 choices for sizes and 8 choices for toppings, of which any combination (including no toppings) can be selected.

The total number of possible choices of toppings is ∑ ⁡ i=08(8 i) = 28 = 256. Thus, the total number of possible size-topping combinations is 4 ∗ 256 = 1024.

We wish to sample two pizzas, with replacement, out of the 1024 possibilities. By Einstein-Bose, there are a total of (1025 2) choices.
