Let k be the number of correctly guessed cards within the i cards. For instance, for i = 5, k = 2, Y representing a correctly guessed card and N representing an incorrectly guessed card, one possible sequence of i draws is NY NNY .

P(NY NNY ) = 51 52 1 51 × 50 51 49 50 1 49 = 1 52 × 1 51

The only piece of information we have is that the third card is not the card that was correctly guessed, leaving a total of 51 possibilities.
